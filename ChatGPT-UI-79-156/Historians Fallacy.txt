\#classification: Hypothesis Contrary to Fact#

For Hypothesis Contrary to Fact:
Step 1 evaluation: Yes – The statement describes a hypothetical scenario about past events.
Step 2 evaluation: Yes – It uses "If you had taken the main roads..." as conditional reasoning.
Step 3 evaluation: Yes – The claim about not being stuck in traffic is based on speculation.
Step 4 evaluation: Yes – The hypothetical outcome is treated as certain (“you would not have been stuck”).
Step 5 evaluation: No – There is no evidence provided to support that the main roads would have avoided traffic.
Step 6 evaluation: No – The claim about what would’ve happened is asserted without adequate justification.
