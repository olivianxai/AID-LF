#classification: Circular Definition#
For Circular Definition:
Step 1 evaluation: Yes - A definition is being presented ("My car broke down because it is no longer working")
Step 2 evaluation: Yes - The definition contains the term itself or a variant ("broke down" is defined using "no longer working" which is essentially the same concept)
