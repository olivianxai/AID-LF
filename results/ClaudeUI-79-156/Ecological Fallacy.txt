#classification: Ecological Fallacy#

Step 1 evaluation: Yes - A statistical claim is made about men as a group scoring better on math than women as a group
Step 2 evaluation: Yes - Jerry, an individual man, is being discussed
Step 3 evaluation: Yes - The argument concludes that Jerry has the same characteristic (being better at math) as the group based solely on the group's characteristic
Step 4 evaluation: No - No substantial evidence or facts are given about Jerry's individual math abilities to support the conclusion
