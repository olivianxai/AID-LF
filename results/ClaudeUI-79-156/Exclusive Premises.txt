#classification: Exclusive Premises#
For Exclusive Premises:
Step 1 evaluation: Yes - The argument is structured as a categorical syllogism with two premises and one conclusion
Step 2 evaluation: Yes - Both premises are negative statements ("No kangaroos are MMA fighters" and "Some MMA fighters are not Mormons")
Step 3 evaluation: Yes - The first premise uses "No X are Y" format
Step 4 evaluation: Yes - The second premise uses "Some Y are not Z" format  
Step 5 evaluation: Yes - A conclusion is reached about the relationship between X (kangaroos) and Z (Mormons)
