#classification: Just Because Fallacy#
#
For Just Because Fallacy:
Step 1 evaluation: Yes - A claim is being made ("I am an idiot")
Step 2 evaluation: Yes - The reasoning provided is expected to justify the conclusion
Step 3 evaluation: Yes - The response provides faulty logical reasoning rather than actual evidence
Step 4 evaluation: Yes - The claimant presents their flawed logic as authoritative
Step 5 evaluation: Yes - No substantive reasoning or evidence is offered to support the claim
